Soojung Kang: The Korean Designer

Soojung Kang or Crystal, is a graphic design student who comes from Korea. She’s a mother of 2: a girl and a boy. Despite her responsibilities as a mother and a wife, I have never seen her miss a class. Hardworking and talented is the two words I use to describe her. We’ve been friends for two years and I have seen many of her works, most of them are unique and remarkable. She is also very easygoing and nice. When I asked her for an interview, she was gladly to help and I learned many things about her.

01.

Why do you want to design?

I wanted to study about design that I used to study in the past. I’m trying to find my talent and dream during study.

02.

What is difficult for you within design and how do you push past that?

Creating new ideas is difficult. So I try to get inspiration from nature, art and kids. Also, I need more practice for developing my drawing skill.

03.

What do you see yourself doing after you finish this program?

I want to be a good mom, wife and carrier woman. I want to work related with typography after graduation

04.

What excites you about design?

Creating new design thing makes me so exciting. Especially, I’ m interested in typography and package design.

05.

What worries you about design?

I need to spend more time to develop presentation about my design in front of audience.

06.

Can design change the world?

In my opinion, design can change many things, from the small things to big things like mood of people or places. I believe that these ideas would lead to great change the world.
